Academic to Action · A Net Zero Journey

Dr. Michelle Tiong, PhD

Turning frontier carbon research into real‑world climate impact — from the laboratory to patents, industry, and people.

A carbon‑technology scientist working across the full arc of the Net Zero transition. As Associate Professor at Xi’an Shiyou University and International Cooperation and Development Director of the Marginal Oil & Gas Development Research Institute — and Secretary General of MAYCHAM CHINA — her work advances CO₂ capture, utilization and storage (CCUS), smart cement, and carbon mineralization, carrying each discovery from lab bench to patents, technology transfer, and real deployment. Recognized in 2025 as a Ten Outstanding Young Malaysians (TOYM) Honoree for Academic Leadership and Achievement.
